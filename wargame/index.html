Battle Calculator

This calculator determines the outcome of a battle between nations. The strength of each nation is represented by their power rating, which is the sum of their military, economic, and political power, on a scale of 1 to 10.

A six-sided dice (d6) is rolled for each nation. A nation wins if their roll is greater than the maximum amount of power (10) minus their own power rating. If the roll is equal to or less than the maximum amount of power minus their own power rating, the nation loses.
